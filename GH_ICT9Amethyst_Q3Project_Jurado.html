'Prevention is better than cure,' and we can agree with that. It's better to stop the disaster from happening rather than having to stop it when it has already occured. In this case, it is fire disasters. Here are 3 things that we recommend you to follow to further prevent fires from happening in your homes.


How to Prevent Fire

Keep extremely flammable items away from heat.		 Objects like paper, rubbing alcohol, nail polish, and even hair should be kept away from the kitchen or highly electric areas to prevent them from catching fire. This might seem excessive, but you can never be too safe when it comes to preventing something as catastrophic as a fire.

Unplug gadgets that are not being used.		 Excessive amounts of electricity can overheat and start random yet dangerous fires. These can come from your beloved gadgets. This can help lessen electricity use as well besides being a good prevention towards fire disasters.

Smoke outside, Not inside the house!		 Although it is already heavily unhealthy, in the unfortunate circumstance that you smoke, please do not do it inside your home. The flame can still ignite within trashcans if it is not properly and fully put out. Be careful when you smoke, both for your home's sake, and your own.
